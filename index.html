Hi, I'm Abhinav, an Engineer based in Maharashtra, India.

I currently work in the Business Excellence domain within the chemical manufacturing sector. Over the years, I have worked across Production, Process Engineering, and Operations Excellence functions, which has given me exposure to both day-to-day plant operations and process improvement initiatives.

I graduated from Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala, and am currently pursuing an ESG certification through Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning.

What I Work On

My professional interests lie at the intersection of manufacturing, data, and continuous improvement.

I enjoy finding practical ways to improve industrial operations using data that is often already available but underutilized. Whether it is production records, maintenance logs, procurement data, or process parameters, I find it fascinating how simple analysis can uncover opportunities for better decision-making.

Outside Work

Away from work, I spend my time exploring a variety of unrelated interests.

I play the keyboard, ukulele, and kalimba, write occasional essays and observations on my blog, follow sports far more closely than I probably should, and occasionally revisit video games that I should have probably outgrown by now.

I have also been a quizzing enthusiast since school and had the opportunity to compete at the national level during my student years.

Curiosity Department

I have a tendency to disappear into rabbit holes on topics that catch my attention.

One week it might be smart manufacturing. The next week it could be world history, cricket statistics, literature, public transport systems, or something completely unrelated.

If you're someone who enjoys learning for the sake of learning, we'll probably find something interesting to talk about.
