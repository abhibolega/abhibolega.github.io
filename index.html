Abhinav Mishra

Engineer • Learner • Musician

Hi, I'm an engineer based in India and associated with the chemical manufacturing sector. I currently work in the Business Excellence domain. Previously, I have worked in Production and Process Engineering roles.

I completed my Engineering degree from Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology and am currently pursuing an ESG certification through Symbiosis Centre for Distance Learning.

Professionally, I am interested in data-driven manufacturing and finding simple ways to improve operations using data that is often already available on the shop floor but remains underutilized. Industrial safety, compliance audits, sustainability, and ESG are some other areas that I enjoy learning about.

Outside of work, I like to play the keyboard, ukulele, and kalimba, read, write occasional thoughts on my blog, and spend time playing FIFA and GTA. I am a quizzing enthusiast and have competed at the national level during school.

I tend to go down rabbit holes on a wide variety of topics, and would be glad to nerd out sometime :).
